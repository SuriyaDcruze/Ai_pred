# Volume 03 — Business Goals

## 1. The market & the users

Indian retail investing has exploded (Groww, Zerodha, tens of millions of demat
accounts). Most retail traders lose money, and the "AI trading" space is dominated by
**overpromising, often non-compliant products** (fake accuracy claims, hidden upsells,
signal-selling without SEBI registration). This is the gap Aegis fills — **not by
predicting better, but by being honest and explainable.**

**Primary users:** Indian swing traders, position traders, long-term investors, and
serious learners who want an analyst that shows its work.

## 2. The value proposition (the moat)

> **Aegis's moat is not accuracy — it is trust.**

We proved (10 experiments) that prediction accuracy is a commodity stuck at ~61% for
everyone. Nobody can sell a real 90%. So competing on "better predictions" is a losing
game. Aegis competes on the one thing the incumbents *refuse* to offer:

- **Transparency** — every number is real, inspectable, reproducible.
- **Explainability** — it shows *why*, and when to *wait*.
- **Honesty** — it states its limits and labels backtest-vs-live.
- **Scientific validation** — decisions are backed by leakage-free evaluation.

A user who understands *why* and trusts the *honesty* is a retained user. Hype churns;
trust compounds.

## 3. Regulatory posture (SEBI) — a first-class business constraint

- Aegis is positioned as **decision-support / education / research**, **not** registered
  investment advice and **not** a research-analyst tips service.
- **No order execution, ever.** No brokerage account is touched.
- **Persistent disclaimers** on every recommendation ("not SEBI-registered advice;
  paper-trade first").
- **Audit trail** of every recommendation shown (Vol 13) — for both trust and compliance.
- If the product ever crosses into "advice for a fee," the compliant path is **SEBI
  Research Analyst registration** — a business decision documented here, not assumed.

## 4. Monetisation — honest options (no path requires lying)

| Model | Fit | Honesty check |
|---|---|---|
| Free education / decision-support tool | ✅ safest | No returns promised; builds trust & audience |
| Freemium (free analysis, paid extras: alerts, portfolio, history) | ✅ | Sell *tooling*, never "profits" |
| Subscription for the intelligence platform | 🟡 | Requires clear "not advice" framing; likely SEBI review |
| Selling signals / "buy this" for a fee | ❌ | Non-compliant + dishonest — the thing we refuse to become |
| B2B (data/API for other honest tools) | 🟡 | Viable once the live track record exists |

**Gate on everything:** no monetisation of "profits" until a **live track record**
(Vol 18) exists. Selling a backtest-only edge is the SpringPad mistake.

## 5. Success metrics (what we actually measure)

**Not** directional accuracy, **not** a leaderboard number, **not** "90%". Instead:

- **Decision quality** — does filtering (Outcome Engine) improve expectancy live?
- **Trade selection** — WIN rate & average R of *taken* trades on the live record.
- **Explainability** — can a user articulate why the AI recommended what it did?
- **Trust / retention** — do users return and rely on it?
- **Reproducibility** — can any claim be re-derived from the pipeline?
- **Robustness** — does the edge hold across markets/regimes/time?

## 6. Strategic sequencing (business)

1. **Prove the edge live** (Vol 18) — the only thing that unlocks any monetisation.
2. **Deepen trust** — richer explainability, portfolio, conversation.
3. **Then** consider a compliant product/subscription with the live record as proof.
4. Real money and marketing come **last**, on evidence — never on a backtest.
